Umeet Bhachu, P.Eng., PMP, CMRP

Rotating Equipment Engineer & Turbomachinery Specialist

Umeet Bhachu is a professional Rotating Equipment Engineer with over 20 years of experience in oil & gas, LNG, and energy infrastructure. His expertise includes pumps, compressors, gas turbines, mechanical seals, lubrication systems, and reliability engineering.

He has authored technical articles in recognized industry publications such as Hydrocarbon Engineering and LNG Industry, and his publications are indexed on Google Scholar.

His current interests include nonlinear causality analysis, transfer entropy, machine learning, and advanced analytics applied to rotating equipment condition monitoring and asset performance optimization.
